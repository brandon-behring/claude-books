# AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations

## Key takeaways

- Builds vocabulary for human–AI collaboration. Centers on the **4D Framework**: **D**elegation, **D**escription, **D**iscernment, **D**iligence — a structured way to think about what to hand to AI, how to brief it, how to evaluate its output, and how to verify the work.
- Introduces the **Description–Discernment feedback loop** as the iterative core of effective AI collaboration. Maps directly to iterative-refinement patterns in the handbook (Ch4: Working Productively, Ch6: Thinking Together).
- Covers generative AI capabilities + limitations, prompting fundamentals, and project planning with AI assistance. Audience: both novices and experienced practitioners (per the course copy, "something for everyone, whether you're new to Claude or a seasoned AI practitioner").
- Co-developed with academic partners at **University College Cork** and **Ringling College of Art and Design**. Includes a final assessment + LinkedIn-shareable certificate of completion.
