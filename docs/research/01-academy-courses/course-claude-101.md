# Claude 101

## Key takeaways

- The **general-user introduction** to claude.ai — the non-engineering counterpart to [[course-claude-code-101]]. Catalog metadata: **12 lectures, 1 hr of video, certificate of completion** (no quiz row in catalog; landing page also does not advertise a quiz count separately from the certificate).
- Curriculum is the broadest of any Academy course — touches the entire **Claude product surface**, not just one lane. Five tracks (paraphrasing section headers):
  1. **Meet Claude** — What is Claude?, Your first conversation, Getting better results.
  2. **Claude desktop app: Chat, Cowork, Code** — single lecture acknowledging the three product surfaces.
  3. **Organizing your work and knowledge** — Projects, Artifacts, Skills.
  4. **Expanding Claude's reach** — Connecting tools (Connectors), Enterprise search, Research mode.
  5. **Putting it all together** — Use cases by role, other ways to work with Claude, conclusion + certificate.
- "Working with skills" appears as a topic for general users, not just engineers — confirms that **Skills** are now positioned as a first-class product feature across the Claude consumer experience (consistent with the unified `claude.ai/directory` rollout per landscape §2.3).
- **"Connecting your tools"** = Connectors (398 verified, per landscape §2.3); **"Enterprise search"** = the org-wide search rollout in Claude for Work; **"Research mode for deep dives"** = the Research mode in claude.ai (multi-step autonomous research over connectors).
- Cert relevance is **D3** (Claude Code Configuration & Workflows: maps loosely via skills/projects) and **D4** (Prompt Engineering: "Getting better results" lesson). Not a primary cert-prep course — better positioned as **onboarding-for-non-engineers** or **prerequisite-for-Claude-Cowork**.

## Open questions / follow-ups

- "Claude desktop app: Chat, Cowork, Code" is a single lecture name — first explicit acknowledgement that the three Claude surfaces are unified in the desktop app. Worth tracking for the handbook's product-surface coverage.
- "Research mode for deep dives" is on the curriculum — depending on rollout state, may map to claude.ai Research or to Claude Managed Agents' Outcomes/Dreaming layer (landscape §5.3).
- Course `© 2025` footer — the **Claude Cowork** product (launched May 2026 per landscape §1.6) is in the curriculum but the page footer predates the broader Cowork rollout, suggesting the course was updated mid-stream.
